Our ROI driven approach

Giovanni Media operates on a strategy designed to maximize our clients’ Return on Investment. This is in part because we pore over multiple strategies and employ advanced targeting algorithms to minimize Cost per Click and maximize lead generation.

Return on Investment (ROI) in social media terms largely relies on the efficiency of the marketing campaign in question. This measure of efficiency is based on each of our client’s goals for each specific advertising campaign, and how well the campaign results stack up to these goals.

Specialized Targeting Measures

You want to ensure your marketing strategy is as efficient as possible. There’s no point in investing in an advertisement when you do not know your customer. Therefore, Giovanni Media keeps track of your customers’ most common similarities and uses this data to target similar audiences.

There is a wide array of targeting features available with Giovanni Media with impressive attention to detail, allowing our clients to systematically refine their audience to each marketing campaign.

This drives up the ROI for each campaign as Cost per Click is reduced even further, thus giving our clients more bang for their buck.

Split Testing

We use a variety of methods to determine the most effective campaign strategy, one of the most effective of these is Split-Testing. This method is used in more experimental or specialized situations, usually when an aspect of a client’s marketing strategy is suspected to be ineffective.

Split-Testing is also utilized when we want to measure results when there are a limited number of variances – by testing each variance.

Advanced Analytics

Assumptions are annoying. With our advanced analytics we’ll be able to see exactly which advertising strategies are working and which aren’t. We use a myriad of metrics including engagements, impressions, reach and many more to tweak marketing strategies and ensure our clients remain successful. Analytics are now more important than ever and the value of a proper analytical framework for your social media marketing campaigns will only increase in importance.

At Giovanni Media, we regularly update our clients with every metric necessary to ensure there is minimal guesswork involved when it comes to your investments.

Remarketing

One of the benefits of our Advanced Analytics is that it allows for Remarketing to target those who may be on the fence about your brand. Remarketing allows one to situate targeted ads to an audience that is defined by some form of previous engagement with your brand. We analyze page clicks, views and several other forms of engagement to retarget audience members who have shown an interest but have yet to commit themselves.

Remarketing campaigns often target customers who have shown an interest in purchasing a particular product by advertising the same product after a period of time, sometimes with a discount.
